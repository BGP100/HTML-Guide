The <b>&lt;blockquote&gt;</b> tag specifies a section that is quoted from another source.
<br>
Browsers usually indent <b>&lt;blockquote&gt;</b> elements (look at example below to see how to remove the indentation).
<pre>
&lt;blockquote cite="http://www.worldwildlife.org/about"&gt;
For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.
&lt;/blockquote&gt;
</pre>

<h1>Default CSS</h1>
Most browsers will display the <b>&lt;blockquote&gt;</b> element with the following default values:
<pre>
blockquote {
  display: block;
  margin-top: 1em;
  margin-bottom: 1em;
  margin-left: 40px;
  margin-right: 40px;
}
</pre>
